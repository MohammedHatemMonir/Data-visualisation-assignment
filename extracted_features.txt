FEATURE DOCUMENTATION & CALCULATIONS

hour:               Hour of the day (0–23). 
                    Logic: Extracted from the 'time' ISO-8601 timestamp.

day_of_week:        The specific day name (e.g., Monday). 
                    Logic: Extracted from the 'time' ISO-8601 timestamp.

is_night:           Binary indicator (1 or 0). 
                    Logic: 1 if hour is >= 20 (8 PM) or <= 6 (6 AM); otherwise 0.

latitude:           North/South geographic coordinate. 
                    Logic: The first numerical value parsed from the 'device_location' string.

longitude:          East/West geographic coordinate. 
                    Logic: The second numerical value parsed from the 'device_location' string.

dew_point:          The temperature ($T_{dp}$) where condensation occurs. 
                    Logic: Calculated using the Magnus-Tetens approximation: 
                    $T_{dp} = \frac{c \gamma}{b - \gamma}$ where $\gamma = \ln(\frac{RH}{100}) + \frac{bT}{c+T}$

humidex:            The "Feels Like" index combining temperature and humidity. 
                    Logic: $T + 0.5555 \times (e - 10)$, where $e$ is vapor pressure 
                    derived from the dew point.

temp_rolling_1h:    1-hour Simple Moving Average (SMA). 
                    Logic: The average of the 4 most recent temperature readings 
                    (since period = 900s), grouped by 'device_id'.
